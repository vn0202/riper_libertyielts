About US

Our Legacy

Established in 1987, Liberty Academy has grown into a trusted leader in the field of professional education and language training. Our commitment to excellence has positioned us as a leading coaching institute in Ahmedabad, specializing in international language assessments and advanced English training.

What We Offer

At LibertyIELTS, we offer a comprehensive suite of instructional services including:

  • IELTS – International English Language Testing System
  • TOEFL – Test of English as a Foreign Language
  • PTE – Pearson Test of English
  • DUOLINGO English Test
  • CELPIP – Canadian English Language Proficiency Index Program
  • General and Advanced English Language Coaching

Our Mission

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality, professional training to prospective students and corporate clients. By adhering to strict ethical standards and fostering a culture of integrity, LibertyIELTS ensures a dynamic and globally competitive learning environment. Our teaching approach is designed to build confidence, clarity, and capability.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate ethically in all our interactions.
  • Excellence: We provide the highest standard of training and support.
  • Accountability: We maintain a legally compliant and responsible learning environment.
  • Commitment: We are devoted to our students’ personal and academic success.

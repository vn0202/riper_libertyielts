1. Information We Collect

When you use our IELTS learning platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email, and contact details when you register or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared only with trusted third-party services (such as analytics and mailing platforms), or as required by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your user experience and remember preferences.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may limit functionality.

5. Data Security

We use modern security technologies, such as SSL encryption, to protect your information. While no method is 100% secure, we strive to ensure the safety of your personal data.

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information.

  • Withdraw consent from receiving marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please email us at libertyacademy.co.in@gmail .

LibertyIELTS is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
